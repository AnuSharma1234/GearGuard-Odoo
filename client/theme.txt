GearGuard follows a dark-theme-only, modern industrial SaaS design that prioritizes clarity, reliability, and operational efficiency. The interface uses deep charcoal and slate backgrounds with subtle surface elevation to create visual separation without high contrast or visual noise. Text is rendered in soft off-white and muted grays to reduce eye strain during long sessions, with a clear typographic hierarchy for headings, metadata, and actions. Color is used strictly functionally: red for critical or overdue states, amber for scheduled or warning states, green for completed or repaired states, and muted gray for inactive or scrapped items. Layouts are grid-based, spacious, and data-first, ensuring complex information such as tables, Kanban boards, and calendars remains easy to scan.
